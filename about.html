My Background

With over a decade of experience leading manufacturing modernization, digital transformation, and operational excellence across Fortune 500 and high-growth environments, I combine deep technical expertise in PLC, SCADA, MES, and IT/OT architecture with the business acumen of an MBA.

My career spans delivering multimillion-dollar automation programs for Procter & Gamble, running large-scale modernization and reliability initiatives at Kraft Heinz and Post Holdings, and founding SolisPLC, a training platform trusted by global manufacturers including Kroger, General Mills, and Danone.

Today, through Joltek, I help engineering leaders, plant managers, and manufacturing executives build scalable, sustainable automation and digital transformation programs that deliver measurable results. My approach blends engineering precision with executive-level strategy.
